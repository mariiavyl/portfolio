About me

I graduated from high school in 2015 and subsequently enrolled at St. Petersburg Electrotechnical University. I completed both my Bachelor's and Master's degrees there, specializing in Technical Systems Management.

In 2018, I embarked on my professional journey by joining a children's programming school, where I nurtured young minds and fostered their interest in coding and technology.

In 2020, I transitioned to working at a design bureau, further honing my technical and management skills. My experience in this role allowed me to work on innovative projects and collaborate with industry experts.

From 2022, I contributed my expertise at Astana Hub, where I was involved in various initiatives aimed at driving technological advancements and supporting start-ups.

As of 2023, I have been residing in Lohja and pursuing further education at Luksia.
